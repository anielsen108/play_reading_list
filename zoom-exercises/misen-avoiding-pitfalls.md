# Zoom Exercise Ideas: Avoiding Major Pitfalls

**Reference:** [Avoiding Major Pitfalls - Misen](https://twitter.com/misen__/status/1354439086828900359)
**Topic:** How play helps avoid major life pitfalls
**Duration:** 5 minutes (Twitter thread)

## Core Themes
- Play as risk-free exploration of possibilities
- Play prevents major life errors through simulation
- Testing boundaries safely
- Learning consequences without permanent damage

## Exercise 1: Play as Safe Experimentation (30 minutes)

Explore how play lets us test dangerous ideas safely

### Activity
1. Identify major life decisions (10 min)
2. Playfully explore options (15 min): Role-play, simulate, imagine
3. Reflection (5 min): What did safe exploration reveal?

---

## Exercise 2: Pitfalls You Avoided Through Play (25 minutes)

Personal stories of play preventing mistakes

### Activity
1. Individual Reflection (10 min): When did play/experimentation save you?
2. Share Stories (12 min): Breakout rooms
3. Patterns (3 min): Common themes

---

## Exercise 3: Boundaries and Consequences (30 minutes)

Use play to explore boundaries without harm

### Activity
1. Identify an area where you're uncertain about boundaries
2. Play out scenarios in breakout rooms
3. Discover consequences through simulation
4. Discuss: What did you learn?

---

## Exercise 4: The No-Play Life (20 minutes)

Imagine life without playful exploration

### Activity
1. Thought Experiment (10 min): How would you make decisions without play?
2. Discussion (10 min): What pitfalls might you hit?

---

## Exercise 5: Designing Play Tests (25 minutes)

Create playful experiments for current life questions

### Activity
1. Identify current uncertainty
2. Design playful way to explore it
3. Share designs
4. Commit to trying one
