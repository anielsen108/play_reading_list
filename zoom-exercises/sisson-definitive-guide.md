# Zoom Exercise Ideas: The Definitive Guide to Play

**Reference:** [The Definitive Guide to Play - Mark Sisson](https://web.archive.org/web/20231220072235/https://www.marksdailyapple.com/the-definitive-guide-to-play/)
**Topic:** Comprehensive guide to adult play; more on why adults should play
**Duration:** 10 minutes

## Core Themes
- Comprehensive benefits of adult play
- Physical, mental, social, emotional dimensions
- Play as health intervention
- Specific play practices for adults
- Integration into modern life

## Exercise 1: Play Benefits Assessment (30 minutes)

Identify benefits you're missing without play

### Activity
1. Current Well-being (10 min): Rate physical, mental, social, emotional
2. Hypothetical Play (10 min): How would play improve each?
3. Evidence Review (10 min): Match to research from Sisson

---

## Exercise 2: Play Prescription (35 minutes)

Create personalized play prescription

### Activity
1. Needs Assessment (10 min): What needs play most?
2. Play Design (20 min): Specific practices for each dimension
3. Integration (5 min): How to fit into life?

---

## Exercise 3: Movement Play on Zoom (25 minutes)

Practice physical play in digital context

### Activity
1. Design Zoom Movement Games (12 min)
2. Play Together (10 min)
3. Reflection (3 min): Can Zoom support physical play?

---

## Exercise 4: Social Play Design (30 minutes)

Create social play opportunities

### Activity
1. Current Social Play (8 min): How much do you have?
2. Design Social Play Events (17 min): Specific plans
3. Commitment (5 min)

---

## Exercise 5: Comprehensive Play Practice (25 minutes)

Integrate all dimensions

### Activity
1. Review Sisson's Framework (8 min)
2. Design Weekly Play Practice (15 min): Physical, mental, social, emotional
3. Accountability (2 min)
