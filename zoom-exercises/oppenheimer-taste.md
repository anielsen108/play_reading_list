# Zoom Exercise Ideas: Frank Oppenheimer on Play as Developing Taste

**Reference:** [Excerpt from Frank Oppenheimer on Play as Developing Taste](https://twitter.com/andy_matuschak/status/1282155960966864897)
**Topic:** Play as how adults hone their sense of taste
**Duration:** 2 minutes (excerpt)

## Core Themes
- Play develops aesthetic discernment
- Adults need play to refine taste
- Taste as cultivated through playful exploration
- Connection between play and judgment
- Play as aesthetic education

## Exercise 1: Taste Development Through Play (30 minutes)

Practice refining taste playfully

### Activity
1. Playful Exposure (15 min): Explore aesthetic domain playfully
2. Taste Refinement (10 min): Notice preferences emerging
3. Reflection (5 min): How did play develop taste?

---

## Exercise 2: Play vs. Study for Taste (25 minutes)

Compare approaches to developing aesthetic judgment

### Activity
1. Studious Approach (8 min): Learn about something formally
2. Playful Approach (8 min): Play with it freely
3. Comparison (9 min): Which refined taste more?

---

## Exercise 3: Your Taste Domains (30 minutes)

Identify where you have refined taste and how it developed

### Activity
1. Taste Inventory (10 min): Where do you have good taste?
2. Origin Analysis (15 min): Did it develop through play?
3. Application (5 min): New domain to develop taste

---

## Exercise 4: Collective Taste Refinement (35 minutes)

Develop shared aesthetic judgment through group play

### Activity
1. Shared Exposure (15 min): Explore together
2. Discuss Preferences (15 min): Compare emerging tastes
3. Synthesis (5 min): Did play enable shared refinement?

---

## Exercise 5: Play as Aesthetic Practice (20 minutes)

Design ongoing practice for taste development

### Activity
1. Choose Domain (5 min)
2. Design Playful Practice (12 min)
3. Commitment (3 min)
